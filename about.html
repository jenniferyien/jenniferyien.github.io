About Me

   Who #Jennifer Yien

  What #Web Developer

Where #New York, NY

 When #Present

   Why #Desire to Learn and Build

I worked as an educator teaching high school for two years, where I developed a new digital arts curriculum and led creation of all marketing collateral. At that time my goal is to increase awareness and foster proper utilization of the arts to aid in the creation of a collaborative society.

After my two years teaching, I wanted to grow my personal skill sets more, so I ventured out and joined an education startup. My primary responsibility was to support users in onboarding and utilizing our product to its full potential. My product knowledge grew and I started to work with the engineering team on certain projects. I began helping test features before release and reporting bugs when I found them, assisting with QA.

The more I continued to work with developers though, the more I have become fascinated by what they can do. I desperately wanted to be able to contribute to the development of a product. I believe that developers are the heart of what makes a product, and at the core of any business. I personally want to learn more and professionally grow my skill sets to be a part of that core and contribute in the most impactful way.
